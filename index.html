About

Mohammed is a researcher, computer scientist, programmer and data scientist who earned his PhD in computer science in 2014 from the University of Missouri. His previous research included predictive modeling, relational cluster analysis, health and nursing informatics. Currently, he is a Postdoctoral Fellow at Emory University Biomedical Informatics working on more exciting projects.
